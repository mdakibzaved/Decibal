    HOW WE WORK

    PREPARATORY PHASE

    In this phase we use manual therapy to work on Pain Management, Restoring Range of Motion, Muscle Flexibility and Length and Symptom Management. It is possessive phase which usually lasts upto 2-3 weeks.

    BUILD UP PHASE

    In this phase we work on Movement Training and Muscle conditioning along with body awareness to enhance one's performance in day-to-day as well as sports-specific activities.

    MAINTENANCE PHASE

    In this phase we focus on preventative care. We prioritize preventative care through personalized exercise regimens, biomechanical optimization, and lifestyle adjustments.
